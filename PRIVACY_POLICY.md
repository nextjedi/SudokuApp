# Privacy Policy for Sudoku Streak

**Last Updated:** November 21, 2025

## Introduction

Sudoku Streak ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

Sudoku Streak is designed to work completely offline and does not:
- Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Track your location
- Access your contacts
- Require account creation or login
- Transmit data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements

## Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device, including:
- Game progress and saved puzzles
- Statistics and streak information
- App settings and preferences

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages as it operates entirely offline without any data collection.

## Third-Party Services

Sudoku Streak does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, used, or shared. All game data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's security measures.

## Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data
- No data is retained by us after uninstallation

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on our store listing. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
